Who Are We

Here at Care Corner, we have built a reputation for regional leadership in medical excellence and innovation
Our scope of healthcare services revolves around patient safety and with our branches in Kandy and Colombo we offer you highly specialized medical care including tertiary and super specialty care at our state-of-the-art facilities.
Our consultants, specialists and employees are dedicated to providing exceptional clinical outcomes and the utmost customer satisfaction

With branches in both Kandy and Colombo, Care Corner brings high-quality healthcare closer to you. Whether you are seeking routine care or specialized treatment, our facilities are designed to offer the best in medical excellence. Discover the difference that compassionate, patient-focused care can make at Care Corner.

At Care Corner, your health is our top priority. Our experienced medical teams are committed to providing personalized care and advanced medical treatments. From emergency services to ongoing health management, we are here to support you every step of the way.
