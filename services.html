Our Services

Explore our Services

From everyday needs to specialty treatment.

Emergency Care

Immediate treatment for urgent medical conditions, including accidents, heart attacks, strokes, and severe infections. Emergency departments are equipped to handle critical situations around the clock.

Laboratory Services

Conducts tests on blood, urine, and other bodily fluids to diagnose conditions, monitor health, and guide treatment decisions. Labs perform tests like blood counts, chemistry panels, and microbial cultures.

Diagnostic Imaging

Utilizes technologies such as X-rays, CT scans, MRIs, and ultrasounds to diagnose and monitor medical conditions. Imaging helps in visualizing internal structures and guiding treatment plans.

Surgical Services

We do a range of procedures from minor surgeries to major operations. We have specialized surgical teams and facilities for different types of surgery, such as orthopedic, cardiovascular, and neurosurgery.

Rehabilitation Services

Provides therapy and support for patients recovering from injury, surgery, or illness. Includes physical therapy, occupational therapy, and speech therapy to aid in regaining function and independence.

Mental Health Services

Povides mental health care, including evaluation, therapy, and treatment for psychiatric conditions. Services can range from inpatient psychiatric care to outpatient counseling and medication management.
